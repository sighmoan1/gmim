Notice from GMIM Advanced Technology Department

This is a prototype of a digital GMIMCoin management system that lets users:

  • See a list of users and their GMIMCoin balances
  • Register an account
  • Login

    What you can do when logged in depends on your role:

  • Loyal Supporters can see how many GMIMcoin they have.
  • Representatives can generate GMIMCoin.
  • CEO can allocate roles to different users and edit GMIMCoin balances.

    Generating GMIMCoin

    When you generate GMIMCoin you get a link for GMIMCoin that can be allocated once to a user.
    The person who gets the link can go to the link, login and claim the GMIM coin
